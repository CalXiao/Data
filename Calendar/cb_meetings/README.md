# Central bank decision dates — NOT procedurally generated

`cb_decision_dates.csv` — policy DECISION/announcement dates (the date the rate
change takes effect from, for meeting-date curve bootstrapping) for the six
banks the pricer covers or will cover: FED, BOE, BOJ, BOC, ECB, RBA.
Compiled 2026-07-06. Like FOMC dates, these cannot be generated by rule —
each bank publishes its schedule ~a year ahead.

## Provenance (per the `note` column)

| bank | 2026 | 2027 | source |
|---|---|---|---|
| FED | ✔ | ✔ | Fed website (same list as `..\fomc\` + the `/fomc` scrape) |
| BOE | ✔ confirmed | ✔ provisional | bankofengland.co.uk/monetary-policy/upcoming-mpc-dates (fetched live) |
| BOJ | ✔ | not yet published | boj.or.jp MPM schedule (fetched live); decision = day 2 of each MPM |
| BOC | ✔ | published ~Aug 2026 | official 2025-08-06 schedule press release (fetched live) |
| ECB | Jul→Dec official; Feb–Jun from press reports (Feb 5 inferred) | ✔ official | ecb.europa.eu GovC calendar (fetched live); decision = day 2 |
| RBA | ⚠ SECONDARY sources only | not yet published | rba.gov.au blocked the fetcher — **verify the 2026 dates on the site before trading off them** |

## Maintenance process (annual, ~August–October)

1. Each bank publishes next year's schedule mid-year (BoC in August, Fed in
   June, BoE/BoJ/ECB/RBA by autumn). Re-fetch the six pages above, append the
   new year's decision dates, and re-tag any provisional dates as confirmed.
2. Keep BOJ/ECB/RBA as the SECOND day of two-day meetings (announcement day).
3. The Fed rows must stay in sync with `..\fomc\fomc_decision_dates.csv`,
   `sofr_curve_feed.py::FOMC_FALLBACK` and `SofrTab.jsx::FOMC_RAW`.

## Consumers / intended use

- Nothing reads this file at runtime YET. It exists for:
  (a) meeting-date front-end bootstrapping per ccy (the FOMC-path logic the
      SOFR tab already has, generalized) — needed for date-precise carry
      around meetings;
  (b) path-based carry vs curve carry (see the 3m carry/roll discussion,
      2026-07-06 session);
  (c) event-risk awareness in the Seasonals stack.
- Related but separate: meeting-dated OIS *prices* (the market's path). USD
  already flows via `USSOFED1..9` on `:8196/fedsofr`. BBG has analogous
  meeting-dated OIS for other ccys — ticker families unverified; wiring them
  into the bridge is future work (same --print-tickers ritual).
